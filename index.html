My Projects.

Price Finder

Web Site

In today's market, consumers fall into two main categories: those who shop on e-commerce platforms like Amazon and Flipkart, and those who prefer the experience of purchasing products locally. A common challenge for local shoppers is the lack of accessible information about product prices in their vicinity. This is where my 'PriceFinder' project steps in. By providing detailed insights into the prices and availability of local products, PriceFinder empowers users to make more informed and cost-effective purchasing decisions. Not only does it serve the interests of consumers, but it also supports local shopkeepers by giving them the opportunity to offer their products at competitive rates, fostering a fair and balanced marketplace Project Link.
